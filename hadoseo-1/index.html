How it actually works

When a crawler or bot visits your app, our edge network intercepts the request at the DNS level. Instead of returning your SPA shell, we spin up a headless Chromium instance, fully execute your JavaScript, and return the completed HTML with all content rendered.

🤖 Googlebotcrawler
→
◉ hadoseo edgeDNS intercept
→
⚙️ Headless renderfull JS exec
→
📄 Rich HTMLreturned

Real users get your original SPA experience — no performance hit whatsoever. Hadoseo only intercepts requests from known crawler User-Agent strings. Your app runs exactly as before. Google just finally sees it.
