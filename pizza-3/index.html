Manifesto · MMXXVI

Peasant
Food.
Royal
Craft.

Pizza was never humble. Born in the volcanic streets of Napoli, refined in its fires, it fed kings and labourers alike. The irony is that the world forgot. We did not. FORNO returns pizza to its rightful position: the highest expression of Italian culinary intelligence. Not a performance. Not a product. A singular act of mastery.

"The dough does not lie. The oven does not forgive. The pizza does not pretend."

The Instrument

The Wood-Fired
Oven as Arena

At 485 °C, a Neapolitan pizza bakes in sixty to ninety seconds. There is no room for hesitation. No margin for error. Our custom-built Vesuvian-stone oven — hewn from the same volcanic tuff that has overlooked Napoli for millennia — becomes your opponent, your partner, and your final judge. You will learn to read fire by instinct.

485 °C Oven Temperature
90 Seconds to Perfect
72 Hours of Immersion

The Three
Acts
of Mastery

Three intensive modules, each designed to deconstruct and rebuild your understanding of Neapolitan pizza — from its geological origins in Campanian volcanic soil to its final, singular expression in wood fire. No shortcuts. No compromises.

01

Module I — Days 1 – 2

The Dough.
The Science of Nothing.

Water, flour, salt, yeast. Four elements that demand a lifetime of study. Master the gluten matrix, the fermentation arc, and the tactile intelligence that no recipe can teach.

02

Module II — Day 2

The Ingredients.
Geography of Flavour.

San Marzano grown in Campanian volcanic soil. Fior di Latte from Agerola. Learn to read an ingredient as a chef reads a landscape.

03

Module III — Days 3 – 4

The Fire.
The Art of the Arena.

The wood-fired oven is not a tool — it is a living system. Learn fire management, temperature reading by eye alone, and the decisive rotation that separates a pizza from a masterpiece.

The Arena.

01× Per Year

FORNO convenes once per year, in September, when the Neapolitan sun softens and the volcanic air carries the promise of perfect fermentation.

XII Participants Only

Twelve seats. No exceptions. The intimacy of a small cohort ensures each participant receives direct mentorship and unrestricted oven time.

72h Immersive Hours

Seventy-two hours of continuous instruction, fire management, fermentation study, and hands-on production. Sleep is optional. Mastery is not.

200+ Years of Tradition

Napoli has been producing the world's finest pizza for over two centuries. We are the latest chapter in an unbroken line of volcanic obsession.
