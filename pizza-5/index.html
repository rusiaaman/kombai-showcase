02

The Ritual

I

The Flour

Six days begin with grain. You will mill your own flour by hand, learning to read its texture before any water is added. There are no shortcuts here — only repetition and humility.

II

The Dough

Hydration is philosophy. We work at 72% — a margin that forgives nothing and rewards everything. Your hands will remember before your mind catches up.

III

The Fire

The oven reaches 485°C. A Neapolitan pizza cooks in 60 seconds or it does not cook at all. You will learn to read fire the way a sailor reads wind — with absolute calm.

The Philosophy

A craft built
on centuries
of silence.

Napoli Nero is not a cooking class. It is a confrontation with your own hands, your patience, and your idea of what a perfect thing can be. You will leave changed — or you will leave early.
