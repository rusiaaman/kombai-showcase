The Craft

The Hands
Do Not Lie.

The dough is a biometric test. It reveals impatience. It records anxiety. It will not forgive a distracted touch or a mind elsewhere. Your hands must be fully present — reading temperature, elasticity, the subtle resistance that signals hydration has reached its correct equilibrium.

We work at 65% hydration. We ferment for a minimum of 24 hours. There are no shortcuts because the shortcuts are visible in the final product — and Napoli has been reading pizza for a thousand years.

The Maestro

"I have eaten pizza on four continents. I have been
insulted on three of them."

Marco Esposito was born in the Quartieri Spagnoli. He trained under the AVPN for eleven years before returning to open a pizzeria that has no menu, no reservations, and no consistent hours. It has a queue that forms at 5am.

He does not teach because he enjoys teaching. He teaches because he finds the current state of Neapolitan pizza outside of Naples professionally offensive, and has decided that intervention is the only honourable response.
