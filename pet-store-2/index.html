Our Story

A love story between
humans and animals.

Petal & Paw was born from a simple belief: the animals who share our lives deserve the same quality of care and nourishment that we seek for ourselves.

We work directly with small-batch producers, certified nutritionists, and humane farms to bring you products that are as good for the planet as they are for your pet.
