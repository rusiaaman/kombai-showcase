The Philosophy

Meet the Founder

I started this studio because I couldn't find what I was looking for — a place that honoured both the discipline and the grace of Pilates. Not a trendy workout, not a spa retreat, but something in between: a space where precision meets warmth, and where every body is treated as a work in progress, not a problem to fix.

Seven years later, this studio is still my life's most intentional work. I am grateful beyond measure for every single person who walks through our doors and trusts us with their body, their time, and their journey.

Elena Vasari
Founder & Lead Instructor, Studio Forma
