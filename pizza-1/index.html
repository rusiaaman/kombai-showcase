Act II

The
Lineage

Three centuries of a craft that did not change. Because it did not need to.

1734
The First Record
Port'Alba, Napoli. The world's first documented pizzeria opens. Flatbread meets fire. No one is impressed yet.
1830
The Permanent Hearth
Fixed ovens replace portable stalls. The craft becomes a discipline. Wood burns at 900°F. Patience becomes method.
1889
The Royal Commission
Raffaele Esposito bakes for Queen Margherita. Basil, mozzarella, tomato. The canon is set. Italy stops arguing. Briefly.
1984
The Codification
Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana founded. Rules written in stone. The dough may not be rolled. This is non-negotiable.
2017
UNESCO Heritage
The art of the Neapolitan pizzaiuolo inscribed as intangible cultural heritage of humanity. The world finally catches up.
Now
Your Turn
Three centuries of knowledge. One evening in a courtyard. Twelve seats remaining. Do not overthink it.
